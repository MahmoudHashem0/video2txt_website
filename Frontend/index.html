Welcome, students, workers from the office and staff of a large library!

Do you have a textbook or some kind of journal from which you need to get the text, but you have no time to print it yourself?

Our service will help you to get a text from a photo or PDF document. After receiving the result, you can download the text and translate it through Google Translate, convert it to a PDF file, or save it in Word format.

OCR or Optical Character Recognition has never been so easy. All you need is to scan or take a photo of the text you need, select the file, and upload it to our text recognition service. If the image with the text was clear enough, you will receive recognized and readable text.

Also you can transform PDF file into images, on output you will get zip archive.

This service does not support hand-written texts.
